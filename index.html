The Qualifier Process

Unlike traditional entrance exams, the qualifier is built into the learning process. You learn for 4 weeks and then prove your competence.

  • 4 Weeks of Content

    Video lectures and assignments for Math, Stats, CT, and English.

  • Weekly Assignments

    Maintain a minimum average to qualify for the final exam.

Eligibility Criteria

Regular Entry

Anyone who passed Class 12 or equivalent.

JEE-based Entry

Qualify JEE Advanced to bypass the 4-week qualifier content.

* Professionals and dropouts are also eligible.
